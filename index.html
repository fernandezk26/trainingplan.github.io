Nutrition Guidelines - Cutting Phase

▼

Regular Days (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri)

1800 calories:

• Protein: 30-35% (135-160g)

• Carbs: 35-40% (160-180g)

• Fat: 25-30% (50-60g)

Speed Days (Wed/Sat)

1800 calories:

• Protein: 30% (135g)

• Carbs: 45% (200g)

• Fat: 25% (50g)

Pre/Post workout carbs!

• Quinoa - Complete protein + carbs

• Brown Rice - Steady energy

• Sweet Potato - Pre/post workout

🥩 Protein Sources

• Chicken - Lean, versatile

• Steak - Iron + B-vitamins

• Salmon - Omega-3s

• White Fish - Ultra lean

🫘 Legumes (Carbs + Protein)

• Black Beans - Fiber + folate

• Red Beans - Potassium

• Green Beans - Low cal volume

• Lentils - Quick cooking

• Chickpeas - Snack/salads

Important Notes & Progressions

• Sunday: Complete rest day

• Swimming: Build from 1500→2500 yards over 8 weeks

• Cycling: Start 60min, increase 10min weekly to 120min

• Half Marathon Taper: Weeks 13-14 reduce volume 40%

• Calorie Reassessment: Every 4 weeks based on energy/progress

• Heart Rate Monitor: Use Polar H10 for accurate zones

• Hydration: Half body weight in ounces + 16-24oz per training hour

• Sleep: 7-9 hours nightly (more during cutting phase)

• Recovery: Listen to your body - scale back if needed
